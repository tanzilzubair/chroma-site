Chroma 1.0

Hello and welcome to Chroma!

Now, before we get to the good stuff, I'd like to take a moment to thank you for choosing to use my little app, and tell you that this choice means a lot to me, and that I won't let you down. With that being said, let's move on to the good stuff!

Do note however, that, since this is the first version of the app publicly available, this isn't going to be a list of changes so much so as a list of features.

Beautiful, responsive UI

Custom, purpose-built designs honed with industry standard best practices and principles, and enriched with all of the trail-by-fire experience I got from Caelum,: built to be smooth, a joy to look at and use, and responsive.

I could talk about it all day, from the carefully chosen colors, to the painstaking process of inlcuding a chosen chroma in specific little nooks of the UI as accents, but I think we'd both much rather that you go and experience it for yourself!

  • A unified, minimal UI with colored accents, beautiful font choices and smooth animations
  • Polished, clean sounds and contextual haptics; carefully made, tested and implemented from the ground up to enrich the user experience: giving more context to available actions and more intuitive feedback on chosen ones.
  • Smooth, fast refresh painting and text editing, blurring the boundary between thinking and putting to paper.
  • An incredibly smooth, animated floating text bar, that appears above the keyboard and gives you your formatting options: from bold text to italics, underlines, strikethroughs, blockquotes, lists, and more!
  • So many tiny and not-so-tiny sized things, we'd be here all day if I listed them; so, go ahead and just enjoy them!

Introducing Scribe & Gloss

That's right, dual custom-built engines, built from the ground up and optimized specifically for fast edits and loads, with seamless autosave, while building in fallbacks, security, reliability and of course, a huge number of features.

Scribe handles all the intricacies (and frustrating complexities) of Chroma's lightweight and incredibly versatile text editor, while Gloss powers Chroma's beautiful painting capabilities and tools.

Taking on this much work at once may have caused me to question my sanity, err several times, but I've come to find that while this results in frustration and pressure in heaps, the innovation and grit it fosters, and the limits I break past while in the process, make it so that when all is said and done, I'd rather not be doing anything else.

  • Scribe, a custom built text parsing, formatting and storing engine, capable of fast edits, continuous efficient autosave, and built with beautiful text displaying capabilities, along with the usual bells and whistles: strong security and reliability, baked in extensibility and modularity (expect new features and options soon!) and of course, a light-weight, mobile friendly footprint to cap it all off.
  • Gloss, the counter-part to Scribe, a fast, lightweight and responsive painting engine with low latency drawing capabilities, a focused set of drawing tools to choose from, and of course, the basics: seamless, continous autosave ensuring you never lose your thoughts, strong security and reliability, and the ability for us to iterate and bring you more features at a blistering rate!
  • Tons of smaller features complementing these powerful engines and making it easier to make them work for you.
  • Many, many smaller and larger changes under the hood, that enable some of the new features coming soon!

The Little Things

  • Inclusive, accessible design, built for everyone. With granular control over a multitude of display options, ensuring Chroma is, above all, comfortable.
  • A nuanced color-blind mode. Chroma is based on color, its magic coming from the vibrancy they offer; we feel it a crime if we did not try our best to make sure everyone could enjoy and come to love it as much as we do.
  • Tons of subtle, contextual animations, a plethora of font family and font size choices, and a minimal formatting mode to ease distractions!
